Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

VaultLog is built around one principle: your data should stay under your control. This privacy policy applies to our marketing website and waitlist form only. The VaultLog app itself does not collect any data.

What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect only your email address. Optionally, you may opt in to receive launch updates.

How we use it

  • To notify you when VaultLog early access is available.
  • To send occasional launch updates if you opted in.
  • To measure interest in VaultLog through anonymous analytics.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your email address.
  • We do not share your email with third-party advertisers.
  • We do not track you across other websites.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with this page. This data is anonymous and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using a browser extension or blocking scripts.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from waitlist emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send. You may also contact us to request deletion of your email from our waitlist.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact us at the support email listed in App Store Connect when VaultLog launches.
