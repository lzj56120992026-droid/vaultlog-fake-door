FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. VaultLog is local-first. Your data stays on your device and is not synced to our servers or any third-party cloud.

Do I need to create an account?

No. There are no accounts, no passwords, and no email signups. Just your device and Face ID / Touch ID.

What if I lose my phone?

Because VaultLog is local-first, we don't have a backup copy. We recommend exporting your vault to your personal backup system (e.g., encrypted local drive) periodically.

Why not just use Apple Notes?

Apple Notes locked notes still live in iCloud. VaultLog never leaves your device — no cloud account, no server, no way for us (or Apple) to see your data.

Can I export my data?

Yes. You can export notes, passwords, and files to standard formats at any time. Your data is always yours.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. 14-day free trial. No payment required to start.

What platforms are supported?

VaultLog will launch on iOS 16+. iPad and macOS support are on the roadmap.
